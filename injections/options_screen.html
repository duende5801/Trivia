How to Play
"So you wanna play pirate do ya!" You've made your way through all of One Eye'd Willie's trials and found his pirate ship. But Ma Ferteli and gang found you and now you must walk the plank! Pick your dificulty and you then will be answering 20 multiple choice questions. Get as many right as you can to help the Goonies escape the cavern. If you get 13 or more questions right you will be able to exit safely. Any less you will be trapped with One Eye'd Willie forever. Use Data's dynomite to blow up wrong answers, but use them wisely, as you only get 5.
